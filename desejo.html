DESEJO

Audiovisuals

DESEJO (portuguese word for DESIRE) is by itself a word full of meaning, which easily arouses emotions. Taking advantage of the potential of the word itself this video intends to bring a new dimensionality to the text through expressive typography.

The desire is responsible for the mutual attraction of the bodies and also for the formation of each letter, which is represented through an expressive body recreation. This attraction / approximation represents the unconsciousness coming from the desire whereas the withdrawal represents the consciousness, which allows to play with the potentiality that the word conveys when related to our personal experiences.

This video was created for an audiovisual representation of typography for the Design + Multimedia Barchelor's Degree at the University of Coimbra.
