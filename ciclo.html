CICLO DE CONVERSAS

Graphic Design / Branding

"Ciclo de Conversas de Design + Multimédia" is a conference consisting of four lecture sessions held annually at the University of Coimbra, having as professional guests linked to Design and other adjacent creative areas.

In order to transpose "Design + Multimedia" to the identity od the event, a concept was reached on the transposition of the pixel to the real world. This way, the use of pixels stamped with ink and paper allowed to make a union of the traditional design with the multimedia, enhancing texture and detail. After digitizing the pixels, the concept was explored through digital tools.

Being the pixel the essence of this work, the acronym CCDM was generated with the stamped pixels, becoming the support of the logo and the other materials. The colors used are shades derived from the pixel's colors — RGB.

All the concept and content was created collectively with Jorge Silva. [ This work was a proposal so all the studios and professionals mentioned are purely hypothetical ]
