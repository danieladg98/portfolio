LIVE PAINTING

Graphic Design

This project aimed to give more color and liveliness to the RIO's BAR — Melgaço, Portugal — and at the same time create two different nights related to art and design. It consisted of two live painting sessions in collaboration with Jorge Cardoso and Juliana Pires. Both illustrations created have the color palette of the bar and its minimalist traits.

The indoors illustration reflects the place where the bar is located through its initials "MLG".Each element represents what better characterizes the county — from the nature (mountains, rails, trees) to the castle and wine.

The counter is characterized by being the place where cocktails are prepared in the summer and therefore, with the same minimalist features of the indoors illustration, it illustrates the bar's main cocktails. For this session a previous restoration work was necessary. The live session at the counter was accompanied by DJ Mosca's set.
