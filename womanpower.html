WOMAN POWER

Graphic Design

"Step on board and let us take you AWAY TO MARS. To a place where the power to create is in people’s hands. Where ideas supersede hierarchy and inspiration can come from anywhere. Any age, continent or background."

Characterized by this definition, the clothing brand AWAY TO MARS launched, in 2017, The T-shirt Challenge. The WOMAN POWER print was one of the winners and thus marketed by the brand during that same year.

This print is an ironic allusion to the ability to overcome any "thorn" that appears on the way.
