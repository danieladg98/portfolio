GAEA

Graphic Design / Editorial / Web

Design is criticism and innovation, it is rethinking all our choices and hypotheses and returning to the beginnings, to our true inspiration. That inspiration began by being nature and evolved to the point where our past creations became the new inspiration and we almost forgot where it all started — in nature.

Will all of these consequences have a direct link to design errors, or did our methods became so vague and rational that they deprived the collection of failures related to our coexistence with the natural environment? This magazine's reflection begins in what can be considered the biggest collective design project of all times — Earth's surface — and it links it with all kinds of fields but mainly with arquitecture and design.

GAEA is an experimental magazine that was created collectively with Ivo Amaro and João Anjos for the Design + Multimedia Barchelor's Degree at the University of Coimbra. It was developed has a printed and online magazine. All the concept and content, except for the articles which were taken from the Whole Earth Catalogue, were created by the team.
