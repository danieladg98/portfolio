LATADA

Branding / Graphic Design

"Festa das Latas e Imposição de Insígnias", better known as "Latada", is part of the academic tradition in Coimbra. Every year, this is the event that marks the reception of the new students in the city. Nowadays, this reception is known for its concerts and other several activities for the students but since the 19th century, freshmen arriving at the University have paraded through the city, with empty cans tied around their ankles.

This tradition led to the whole branding of the event being based on the cans. Their representated in 3D to enable the creation of cans in real scale for the activities and where the logo is used as a label — this way a unique can is representative of the whole event without needing any additional information.

Since each course has a distinctive color, the colors chosen to represent them all in a neutral way were the black and the white. Being black the junction of all colors and white the absence of color, they reflect the university as a whole.

Created alongside with the rest of the Image Team of the event (Carina Leitão, David Brazette, Gonçalo Correira, Juliana Pires, Renata Pereira).
